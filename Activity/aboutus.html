What We Do?

The Smile Revolution(TSR) is not an organization but a family of enthusiasts who practice austerity in
every breath they take. We at TSR love to work for children and thus making them happy.
We reach out to children who do not belong to affluent families and provide them what they need
We celebrate with them, play with them infact live with them.
TSR is a non profit organization, we are happy to inform you that we get immense support. 😇
